Ski or ride with friends & save

If you’ve got your sights set on a heliski trip in 2027, this is your calling. We have prepared (what we hope is) an easy to understand group incentive model that rewards your efforts for gathering the clan.

Group Offer Tiers & Terms

Group of 4 = Receive 1/2 of a Free Seat
Group of 8 = Receive 1 Free Seat
Group of 12 = Receive 2 Free Seats

Who is eligible for the offer?
Whether you’re a seasoned Last Frontier Heli convert, or a first time guest, this offer is open to anyone.

Does the offer expire? 
No. But it would be wise to lock in your preferred dates soon as the offer is limited to 12 seats per tour.

Can the discount be shared?
Yes. The discount can be shared equally amongst group members, or applied to a single person.

How do I apply?
Fill out the form below and select one of the tours from the drop-down menu.

Terms & Conditions

Discount Terms

  • Group sizes need to be maintained in order to receive the group discount. The free seat[s] or partial free seat in the case of a group of four, will be applied at the time of final payment 90 days prior to the trip start date. For example, if eight people pay deposits for a trip but only seven can travel, unless they find a replacement, they’ll only qualify for a four-person discount (half a free seat), not a full one.
  • Group discount not combinable with other offers including our new guest referral credit.
  • Vertical Refunds will only be given on full paying seats. No refund will be given on the free seats.
    Extra Vertical charges apply to all seats [paid or free].
